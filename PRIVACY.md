# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 16 March 2026

## Data Collection

My apps do not collect, store, or process any personal data or information from you.
No cookies, analytics, tracking tools, or third-party services are used to gather information about you.
Any data required to operate my apps are stored on-device.

## Third-Party Services

When you download or use my apps through the App Store, Apple may collect information as described in their privacy policy.
For more information, please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

When you login to a third-party service through my app (such as a service that is required to use my app's functionalities), that service may collect information as described in their privacy policy.
For more information, please refer to the service's privacy policy on their official website.
My apps will never collect and send this information to any remote server for storage.

## Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. 
Any changes will be trackable via Git commits, and the effective date will be updated.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue in this repository.
